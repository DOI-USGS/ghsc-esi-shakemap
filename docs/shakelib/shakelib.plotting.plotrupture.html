shakelib.plotting.plotrupture¶

shakelib.plotting.plotrupture.plot_rupture_wire3d(rupture, ax=None)[source]¶

  • rupture – A Rupture instance.

  • ax – A matplotlib axis (optional).

Returns

Matplotlib axis.
